Registration Help 
-----------------------------------

The registration system allows a user to register to the chat server so that
they can enter/create other chat rooms and have access to commands reserved for 
registered users.


/REG    - Enter the email, nick name, and full name to obtain a temporary password.

/LOGIN  - Log onto the system in order to get out of the lobby.  

/PASS   - Allows you to change your password

/RESEND - Resend the verification email

/UPDATE - Update your profile

/VERIFY - Verify your email address
