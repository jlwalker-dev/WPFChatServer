Moderator Help
-----------------------------------

A modertator is a user who has been elevated by the room's administrator
and has the ability to kick or ban a user from the room.

ROOM commands avaialble to moderators

/KICK    Kicks the user from the room and they receive the included message.

/BAN     Bans the user from the room.

/FORUM   Used to start or stop a room's forum

/RMLOAD  Load a room profile and enter the room

End of Moderator help.
